# FundSync

## Users
- **Primary Audience:** B2B Sales, Fundraisers, Partnerships Managers, and Startup Founders.
- **Context:** Working in high-stakes, fast-paced environments where standard pitch decks need to be personalized rapidly to close deals. Often using modern browsers on MacBooks or high-end monitors.
- **Mindset:** Stressed but ambitious. They want tools that feel like magic, save them hours of manual PowerPoint editing, and make them look incredibly thoughtful and prepared to their sponsors.

## Product Purpose
- **Core Value:** Automates the hyper-personalization of pitch decks (.pptx). It recursively reads slide text, scrapes a target sponsor's website for CSR/technical mandates, and uses AI to rewrite the deck's copy to perfectly align with that specific sponsor's goals.
- **Key Flow:** Upload Master Deck -> Input Sponsor URL -> Adjust Tone -> AI Generates Diff -> User Reviews/Approves -> Download Personalized Deck.

## Tone & Voice
- **Voice:** High-Agency, precise, deterministic, and slightly editorial.
- **Vibe:** "Cockpit meets Art Gallery." It should feel like an expensive, custom-built financial tool, not a generic SaaS template. 

## Anti-References
- Generic "Stripe-clone" SaaS (no purple gradients, no floating isometric illustrations).
- Boring enterprise software (no dense, unstyled data tables, no standard Bootstrap blue links).
